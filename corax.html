Solution

Corax life box - An armor to protect the defenseless

An innovative, flexible and lightweight tent that allows the treatment and safe transportation of patients with severe burns or low immune defenses. It creates an aseptic and controlled micro-environment, reproducing in a transportable and low-cost device the features of a room of Burn Intensive Care Unit (BICU). The flexible part is washable and there are no disposables, solving the problem of supplies and disposal. Our device controls the chemical and physical properties of the air, temperature, humidity preventing infections that are the main cause of death.
