BioVerse

Care accessible anywhere

A revolutionary approach

Bioverse develops new biomedical technologies for emergency and developing contexts. Our team of engineers believes that the development of new technologies that start from the real needs and from the analysis of the resource, can reduce the gap in health care between the countries of the world.

In the era of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), the health care discrepancy between countries is not acceptable. To satisfy the SDG3, Bioverse wants to fight the critical components of public health, in particular with objective 3.2 to prevent infant mortality.
