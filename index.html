A revolutionary approach

Bioverse develops new biomedical technologies for emergency and developing contexts. Our team of engineers believes that the development of new technologies starting from the real needs and an accurate analysis of the resources, can reduce the gap in health care between the different world areas.

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), clearly indicate that the health care discrepancy between countries is not acceptable. To satisfy the SDG3, Bioverse aims to fight the critical components of public health, in particular with objective 3.2 in mind, that of preventing infant mortality.

Our starting project is Corax
